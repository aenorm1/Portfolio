US Paralympic Rowing Team

As a volunteer with the US Paralympic Rowing Team, I had the opportunity to work directly with adaptive athletes, supporting their training and competition preparation. My role involved assisting with equipment setup, providing logistical support during training sessions, and helping to create an inclusive and supportive environment for the athletes. This experience gave me valuable insights into adaptive sports and the importance of accessibility in athletics.

Spaulding Rehabilitation Center

At Spaulding Rehabilitation Center, I volunteered in the physical therapy department, working alongside healthcare professionals to support patients in their recovery journey. My responsibilities included assisting with patient mobility exercises, helping to maintain a clean and organized therapy environment, and providing emotional support to patients during their rehabilitation process. This experience deepened my understanding of patient care and the importance of rehabilitation in healthcare.

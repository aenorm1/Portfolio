About Me

I am a graduate student at Boston University working toward a Master's of Science in Biomedical Engineering. I received my Bachelor's from Wentworth Institute of Technology with a major in Biomedical Engineering and a minor in Chemistry. I was born in Newton Massachusetts, although both my parents are from South Africa. I grew up in an environment where multiculturalism and racial diversity is encouraged and promoted. I am a well rounded candidate with computer programming and wet lab experience. I have experience relating to biomechatronics, animal cultures, cell cultures, analytical chemistry methods. I also have clinical experience from time working as an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT). After graduation, I hope to pursue further academic merit.

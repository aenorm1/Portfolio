Total Control

For my Senior Design Project, I collaborated with two other lab partners to design a modular gaming controller using woodshop, 3D printing, and Arduino coding. I focused on the mechanical aspects of the project handling the design of the case, and the CAD designs of the different parts that make up the controller. I created several SOLIDWORKS drawings laying out my design ideas, used woodshop to manufacture the case, and utilized FormLabs 3D printing to add the modular components to the case.
